This is Musician's Mentor, the ultimate free resource to help young musicians win their next audition.

We are a team of accomplished high school, college, and professional musicians who have put together a guide of common audition material for each instrument. Each guide includes standard excerpts, explanations, and related resources that can give you an edge in your preparation. All excerpt guides were put together by musicians who have successfuly prepared these excerpts for major youth auditions.
